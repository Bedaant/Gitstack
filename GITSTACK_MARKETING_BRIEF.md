# GitStack Marketing Brief
## For Marketing Team — Use This To Create Ads, Landing Pages, Emails, Social Posts

---

## One-Liner (Use This Everywhere)

> **GitStack is the AI platform that helps non-technical founders discover, understand, and deploy free open-source alternatives to expensive SaaS tools — complete with copy-paste setup commands and AI-ready build prompts.**

---

## The Problem We Solve

Founders and small businesses pay $300–$2,000/month for SaaS tools (CRM, email, analytics, scheduling, etc.) when free, open-source alternatives exist. But GitHub is intimidating, READMEs are written for developers, and nobody tells you which tools work together.

**GitStack bridges that gap.**

## What We Build (Feature Breakdown)

### 🧩 CORE FEATURES (The Big Three)

| Feature | What It Does | Marketing Angle |

| **Stack Generator** | Describe your idea ("I need a CRM"). Get 4-6 curated open-source tools that work together. | "From idea to tech stack in 10 seconds" |
